The Hunger Games Simulator

Date: September 1, 2024

The Hunger Games Simulator lets you watch a virtual Hunger Games play out with randomized tributes and dynamic events.

Key Features:

  • Tribute Generation: 24 unique tributes are created with randomized names, traits, and appearances.
  • Dynamic Events: Fights, alliances, and unexpected deaths happen each hour. Weather and disasters add to the unpredictability.
  • Real-Time Stats: See how many tributes remain, track the most kills, and find out who the fan favorite is.
  • Controls & Settings: Start, pause, or restart the simulation. Adjust event frequency and toggle dark mode.
  • Victor Log: A record of past Hunger Games victors is saved for reference.

It's a fast-paced, unpredictable arena where anything can happen!
