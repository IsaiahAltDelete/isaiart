A Guide to Evading Reddit Bans

Introduction

If you've been banned on Reddit, it can be challenging to create new accounts without getting flagged again. However, by using tools such as Gmail's alias trick and a VPN like NordVPN, it's possible to bypass bans and start fresh. This guide will walk you through these methods, helping you avoid detection while setting up new Reddit accounts.

Step 1: Utilizing the Gmail Alias or Dot Trick

Gmail allows you to create unlimited variations of your email address, all of which will be directed to your main inbox. This is useful for creating new accounts without being flagged for reusing an old email.

  • Plus Alias: Add a '+' followed by any word before the '@' in your Gmail address (e.g., yourname+reddit@gmail.com), and Gmail will still treat it as your primary email.
  • Dot Trick: Gmail ignores dots in email addresses. For example, yourname@gmail.com and your.name@gmail.com are treated as the same account, but Reddit will see them as different.

Step 2: Using NordVPN to Mask Your IP Address

Reddit often flags accounts based on the IP address they were created from. By using a VPN, you can avoid being linked to your old, banned accounts.

  • IP Bypass: VPNs allow you to route your internet connection through different servers, changing your IP address. This helps avoid Reddit detecting multiple accounts from the same IP.
  • Privacy Protection: NordVPN hides your real location, which adds an extra layer of anonymity when creating new accounts.

Step 3: Combining the Gmail Trick and VPN

By combining the Gmail alias or dot trick with NordVPN, you can significantly reduce the chances of Reddit linking your new accounts to your banned ones.

  • Create a New Email: Start by generating a new Gmail alias or variation for each account (e.g., yourname+newreddit@gmail.com).
  • Connect to a VPN: Make sure you're connected to NordVPN (or another reliable VPN) before creating the account, ensuring Reddit sees a new IP address.

Best Practices for Avoiding Detection

While the above steps are effective, there are additional precautions you should take to minimize the risk of being banned again:

  • Clear Cookies and Cache: Before creating new accounts, always clear your browser’s cookies and cache to remove any traces of previous activity. You can also use an incognito or private window for added protection.
  • Rotate VPN Servers: Don’t rely on the same VPN server repeatedly. Reddit may flag IPs that are used too often for new account creations. Regularly switch servers to keep your activity from looking suspicious.
  • Use Unique Usernames: Avoid using similar usernames across different accounts. The more distinct your username, the less likely it is to raise red flags.
  • Vary Your Behavior: Be mindful of your actions on Reddit. If you continue the behavior that led to your previous ban, you risk getting flagged again, even with the technical precautions.

Conclusion

By following these steps and best practices, you can effectively avoid Reddit bans and create new accounts with greater anonymity. Remember to always clear your browser history, use a VPN, and vary your online behavior to minimize the chances of detection. Stay smart and cautious!
